Puppies

All our puppies comes with:

  • veterinary check
  • vaccines
  • micro chip
  • multiple deworming treatments
  • registering in Serbian Kennel Club or Macedonian Kennel Club
  • export pedigree FCI and all other documents
  • detailed insight and report about puppies treatment, health care, information about type of food and vitamins program given to puppies during the time spent under our supervision in our kennel before exporting
  • possibility of home delivery by car or air transport to any country
  • proper grooming treatment before exporting
  • ownership contract with possibilities of co-ownership and other arrangements
  • full education about the breed and needed care and treatment for puppies
  • all additional veterinary analyses and advices in general if needed on demand
  • future friendship, help and contact for life with our kennel
